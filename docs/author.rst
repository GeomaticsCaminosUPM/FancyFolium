Author
======

FancyFolium is developed by **Miguel Ureña Pliego**, as part of the
`Advanced Geomatics Research Group (AGA) <https://blogs.upm.es/aga/en/>`_ at the
`Universidad Politécnica de Madrid <https://www.upm.es/>`_. The package grew out of
the group's need to visualize geospatial research data - building-footprint
surveys, structural/damage assessments, raster models - as shareable, interactive
maps for both technical and non-technical audiences.
